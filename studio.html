The Architect's Voice

HPA was founded by Ar. Hiren Patel in 1989, the young architect took upon himself to create a better world through his unique art and designs. From the very beginning, HPA has been passionate about creating spaces that inspire and transform the way the world perceives art. The firm has now grown exponentially with over three decades of experience in the field of architecture, interior design, and landscape design. At HPA, it is a constant strive to break the conventional ideas and practices of design and strive for the future.

Our Vision

Ar. Hiren Patel is one of India's most celebrated architects. He completed his architectural studies in 1983 from CEPT University and that led to the beginning of a legendary journey. Patel founded HPA in 1989 after completing his intense studies in landscape design. It is remarkable how he has been drawn to the world of art and design since childhood. Working with inspiring artists from around the world, his art and design projects seamlessly intertwine.

The young architect had always been truthful to his work and determined to serve the society the best he could. He realised the power of art to change the world quite early in his career. His oeuvre, philosophy, and ideas have evolved with the times and he believes that architecture is not only about buildings, it is about shaping people's lives. And thus, his journey of seeking the truth through his art is eternal.
